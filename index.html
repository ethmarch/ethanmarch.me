About Me

I'm a third year student at Northeastern University studying Data Science. Projects

Below I've featured a few projects I've worked on that I'm most proud of. For more of my work, check out my GitHub!


DataCuse: Syracuse's Open Data Portal


During my internship with the City of Syracuse I assisted with the creation of the city's first open data portal. The site is built on ESRI's Open Data platform. I contributed to the front-end design of the site, created tutorials for users, and automated data retrieval, cleaning and update processes.




Word Frequency Twitter Bot


This is a Twitter bot I created using Python and the Twitter API. Users can tweet a word at it and it will then find how many of their last 1000 tweets they've used that word in and reply to them with the frequency. I'm currently in the process of moving the hosting of the bot to Heroku.
